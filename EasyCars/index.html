Terms & Conditions

Everything you need to know about your rental.

Driver Requirements

  • Minimum age is 22 years.
  • Minimum driving experience is 3 years.
  • Valid Category B license required (IDP required if outside EU).
  • Passport or EU ID card must be presented at pick-up.

Payment & Deposit

  • Payment via bank transfer (pre-pay) or Credit/Debit card at pick-up (VISA, MasterCard, AMEX).
  • No cash payments accepted. No Maestro cards.
  • No cards from mobile-only banks (e.g., Revolut, N26, PaySera) for the security deposit.
  • Deposit is refunded in 5-10 calendar days.

Insurance

Standard Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) is included. The excess ranges from €500 to €2000 depending on the vehicle class.

Full insurance (0.00 EUR excess) is available as an extra service for €20/day.
